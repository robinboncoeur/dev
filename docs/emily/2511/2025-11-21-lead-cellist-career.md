From everything I can find, the solo cellist you’re thinking of is **Loïs van Malenstein**.

### How we can be confident it’s her  
- The Haydn Jeugd Strijkorkest’s commercial release *Haydn Jeugd Strijkorkest 2017* includes **Carl Reinecke – Serenade for Strings in G minor, Op. 242**, credited explicitly as *“Serenade for Strings in G minor (feat. Loïs van Malenstein)”* or *“Carl Reinecke, Haydn Jeugd Strijkorkest HJSO, Loïs van Malenstein”* on multiple streaming platforms. citeturn2search6turn2search8turn2search9

- The orchestra’s own 2017 annual report lists her among the cellists for the Reinecke Serenade in G minor on that tour/repertoire. citeturn2search18

### Did she continue her career as a cellist?

Yes, she absolutely did—and still does.

Public information shows that:

- She studied classical cello at the **Prins Claus Conservatorium** in Groningen. citeturn2search0
